About ADNSU

Azerbaijan State Oil and Industry University (ADNSU) is one of the leading technical universities in the Caucasus region, specializing in oil engineering, chemical technology, and industrial innovation.

Founded in 1920, ADNSU has a rich history of academic excellence and has produced thousands of specialists who have contributed significantly to the oil industry and technological development of Azerbaijan.
